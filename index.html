Challenge: London's housing market is complex and highly influenced by crime rates, making it difficult for buyers to identify safe, undervalued neighborhoods.

Solution: Developed a comprehensive spatio-temporal analytics pipeline that merged over 1 million housing transaction records with detailed crime statistics.
